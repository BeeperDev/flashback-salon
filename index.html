A Little About Me...

I was born and raised in Northern Virginia. I got hooked on hairstyling when I started taking cosmetology classes in high school. Styling hair has been my career ever since! I've spent years in multiple shops surrounded by cutting shears and color swatches. After decades behind the chair with hundreds of customers under my blades and attending more hair shows and conventions than I can count, I've gained many skills perfecting my craft. In 2011, I became my own boss and have my own studio in Hedgesville, WV. Everyday I continue to push myself to dream up fresh ideas and experiment with the latest trends.

Top Services

From just a trim to a complete color, highlight, cut, and style transformation...
I'm here to help you customize your unique look!

  • Color + Cut + Style

    This is the works! I live for creating shapes that play up your features with the benefit of easy styling. Full highlight and balayage + hair cut or more!

  • Full Highlights

    Remember when you were a kid and at the end of the summer you had all those different variations of tones in your hair? That's what some full highlights will give you.

  • Balayage + Style

    Calling all my low-maintenance fashion babes! This is a freehand lightening technique that starts away from the scalp to give you up to four months of beautiful maintenance free color!

  • Transformative

    Are you looking for something completely different? Wanting vivid fashion colors? Looking to be the blondest you can be? This might be the service you are looking for. Typically includes a full head of babylights, with a tip out on the ends, or a platinum card, or global lightening.
